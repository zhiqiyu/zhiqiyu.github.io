Hyperspectral Remote Sensing for Geospatial Intelligence

26/Oct 2017 3 min. read

GeoINT Remote Sensing

This article is for an assignment of GGS684 Topics in Geospatial Intelligence. I believe the Hyperspectral remote sensing will be the game changer for geospatial intelligence. Traditional remote sensing sensors usually provide only 10s of bands that cover visible spectrum range and near infrared range. The limited number of bands lead to the relatively broad range of spectrum that a band would cover. But hyperspectral sensors greatly shrinks the spectrum range that one band cover so that each band can ’focus’ more on its designated spectrum range.
